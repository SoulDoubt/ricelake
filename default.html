Welcome to Rice Lake Family Campground

Spend a day or make it home for the summer! Our campground offers a scenic environment, a multitude of nature trails and much more! We are a full-service facility, featuring 60 campsites, three all-season cottages and one summer only cottage on 16 acres of mature forest. We can accommodate groups of all sizes with our 1, 2, and 3 bedroom cabins. Plan your next getaway for a month, a week or even just a day at Rice Lake Family Campground.
