About Us

Gaston and Louise Forget relocated to Rice Lake in the fall of 2008, where they fell in love with the area’s serene environment, plush scenery and natural privacy. The couple’s newfound passion led them to the long-standing Rice Lake Resort, a full-service facility offering 60 campsites, four all-season cottages, a multitude of trails and much more. Since the Forget’s acquisition, they have increased the sites lot size, installed a water well with filtration system (water softener, filter, UV, chlorine), renovated the three original cabins and built a brand new three bedroom cabin. As the resort grows, the Forget’s will be adding a chip stand, new showers, a new laundry facility and a recreation hall for social gatherings, family reunions, weddings, etc. Come experience Rice Lake with us!
